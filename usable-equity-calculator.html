How much equity can you actually use?

Your home may be quietly funding your next purchase. See your usable equity (to 80% of value) and an indicative budget for the next property.

How it works: lenders typically let you borrow up to 80% of your property's value without LMI. Usable equity = (value × 80%) minus your current loan. Used as a deposit plus costs, that equity can fund a much larger next purchase — subject to your income and serviceability.
