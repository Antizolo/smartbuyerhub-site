Frequently asked questions

Mortgage broking, answered.

Does it cost anything to use Smart Buyer Hub?
Your initial strategy session is free with no obligation. Mortgage brokers are typically paid a commission by the lender once your loan settles, so there's usually no direct cost to you for the broking service.
How many lenders do you compare?
We compare a panel of more than 30 lenders — including the Big 4 banks plus specialist and non-bank lenders — across thousands of home loan products to match your situation.
Can I buy with less than a 20% deposit?
Yes. Many buyers purchase with a 5–10% deposit. Options include the First Home Guarantee (5% deposit, no LMI), low-deposit loans with Lenders Mortgage Insurance, and guarantor loans using a family member's equity.
What areas do you serve?
We're based in Greater Sydney and help clients Australia-wide, with digital appointments via Zoom or Google Meet — seven days a week, including after hours.
How are you different from going straight to my bank?
A bank can only offer its own products. We're independent and compare 30+ lenders to find the most suitable option for your goals, then manage the application and lender negotiations on your behalf.
How quickly can I get pre-approved?
We can often arrange pre-approval within a few business days once your documents are ready, helping you move quickly in a competitive market.
